STATUE OF UNITY

The Statue of Unity is the tallest statue in the world. It is located in India, in the state of Gujarat. This statue is a tribute to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, a great Indian leader. The statue of Unity is about 182 meters or 597 feet high and seems to touch the sky.

INDIA GATE

India Gate is a historic monument located in New Delhi, India's capital. It was designed to pay tribute to the soldiers who bravely battled during the First World War. On the India Gate memorial, the names of soldiers who were sacrificed during the battle are engraved.

KEDARNATH

Kedarnath is believed to be the place where Lord Shiva, one of the major deities in Hinduism, resides. It is said that the temple of Kedarnath was built by the Pandavas, the heroes of the Indian epic Mahabharata, to seek the blessings of Lord Shiva.
